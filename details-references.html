ACC/AHA Heart Failure Guidelines 2022

About This Reference
"This comprehensive guideline provides a 2022 update for the management of patients with heart failure. It replaces the 2013 ACCF/AHA guideline and the 2017 focused update. The document emphasizes Guideline-Directed Medical Therapy (GDMT) for all stages, specifically focusing on the new 'Four Pillars' of treatment to improve survival rates and reduce hospitalization for HFrEF and HFpEF patients."This comprehensive guideline provides a 2022 update for the management of patients with heart failure.

Protocol Update Emergency Department

New Mass Casualty Protocol Implemented Across All Emergency Units

Dr. Ahmad Nassar Apr 8, 2026 4 min read

The Emergency Department has adopted an updated mass casualty protocol to improve response time, triage quality, and inter-team coordination during high-impact incidents.

The revised protocol introduces a clearer command structure during the first response window and defines explicit role assignments for triage, airway, circulation, and documentation teams.

A major improvement is the new communication escalation ladder, which reduces handover delay between resuscitation, imaging, surgery, and intensive care services when patient volumes increase rapidly.

To support implementation, the department will run structured simulation drills over the next six weeks, followed by an audit cycle focused on activation time, treatment initiation, and transfer intervals.
